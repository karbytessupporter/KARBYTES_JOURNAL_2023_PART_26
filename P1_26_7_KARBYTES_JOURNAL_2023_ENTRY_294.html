Today karbytes subscribed to its second Patreon membership (and each membership involves my debit card getting deducted some fixed amount of money on the first day of each month in which that membership lasts). The content creators which I support on Patreon are among my greatest sources of inspiration (and I generally enjoy reading the comments which their fans leave online). It should be noted that I (i.e. karbytes) also have a Patreon page (but I admit I am a bit dismayed to see that not a single person has yet signed up to sponsor me; not even the bit-tier membership which only asks for a measly dollar per month because what I want more than financial support from other people is acknowledgement that my content is discover-able and maybe also appreciated by people other than myself (and the link to my Patreon page is on the home page of this website and on the home page of my primary website).
